Dibaiê dibaiô kuraîquidê î ô  
Dekû kurakauná nake ibutuaê Ikuranê debaiê  
Dibaiê dibaiô kuraîquidê î ô  
Dibaiêi oabaî oianamaba yaiuú yaba uô uô  
  
Day by day we walk on.  
Our feet pressing the same earth  
Our fathers and their fathers' fathers  
walked, day by day, while they walked on.  
Day by day we walk on.  
Hush, little ones,  
Don't tell the earth we love her yet—  
We will sleep in her soon enough,  
but for now, we walk on.
